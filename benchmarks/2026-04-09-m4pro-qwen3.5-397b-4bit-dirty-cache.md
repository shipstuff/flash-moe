## Key Findings

1. malloc-cache-64: +87.6% (8.28->15.54 tok/s) — BEST config so far
   - Works despite packed_experts being zero-filled (OS page cache on safetensors)
   
2. **CRITICAL BUG: packed_experts/*.bin are all zeros**
   - Every expert buffer dispatched to GPU shows first8=[00000000 00000000]
   - GPU is computing on zero-filled garbage and still producing "correct" output
   - Must fix repack_experts.py and re-run — real numbers likely MUCH higher

3. 2bit reduces TTFT by ~26% (1100ms -> ~810ms)
